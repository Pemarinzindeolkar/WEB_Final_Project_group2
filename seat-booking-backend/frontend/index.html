How it works:

  • Each table has 2 chairs
  • Book using your Student ID (Format: STUXXXXXXXX)
  • You have 30 minutes to reach your seat after booking
  • Seat expires automatically if not reached within 30 minutes
  • Admin can manually remove bookings
