Library Rules & Guidelines

  • 1Students must carry their college ID card at all times inside the library.
  • 2Bookings are limited to 2 seats per day. Plan your visit accordingly.
  • 3Unverified bookings will expire automatically after 30 minutes.
  • 4Maintain silence at all times. Mobile phones must be kept on silent mode.
  • 5Food and beverages are strictly prohibited inside the library.
  • 6Keep your seat and the surrounding area clean and tidy.
  • 7Cancel your booking if you are unable to attend — do not leave seats vacant.
  • 8Library property must be handled with care. Report any damage immediately.
  • 9Future-date bookings require admin approval before they are confirmed.
  • 10Violation of rules may result in suspension of booking privileges.
Same-day bookings are auto-verified. Future bookings await admin approval.
